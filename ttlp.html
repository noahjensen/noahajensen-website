Through The Lens

Logo and Identity Redesign

About This Project

ï¿¼I was asked by a local photography studio to update their logo and communication systems (such as their letterhead, business cards, etc.). Their old logo looked very unprofessional and was very hard to read. My goal on this project was to add a sense of "motion" to the logo as their name represents a sense of movement in the form of light. I also wanted to simplify the old logo as the antique box camera of the old logo overwhelmed its viewers. I took away the illegible cursive font from the old logo and updated it to something much more modern.
