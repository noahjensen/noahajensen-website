About Me

Hello there! My name is Noah Jensen and I am a 26 year old graphic designer & front end developer from the Little Rock, Arkansas area. I'm a graduate from the University of Minnesota Twin Cities with a bachelor of fine arts in graphic design.
I have over 3 years of professional working experience working with dozens of clients - both big and small. Feel free to check out my résumé.

I've had an interest in artistic expressions for as long as I can remember. Growing up, my passion was always related to film. The designs of movie posters often influenced my decision to go see that particular film. It wasn't until college that I discovered my interest in graphic design and front end development. How could something as simple as a movie poster influence a customer's mind about which movie to see? It's thoughts such as these that helped me think more broadly about graphic design as a whole and its impact on society. Without design and those seeking to furthur its purpose, life would be a drastically different story. I will always seek to improve the quality of graphic design, whether that involves me sketching, drawing, printing, or designing on my computer.
