Senior Thesis Book

About This Project

ï¿¼This was my senior thesis project made during the fall semester of 2014 at The University of Minnesota Twin Cities. It took the form of an educational booklet explaining how YouTube offers organizations, businesses, websites, and content creators the assets needed to either flourish or fail socially. The website has drastically changed the lives of many businesses, organizations, and content creators. It has helped other websites become successful, while also leading others to their dissolution and demise. I really wanted the book to be used by any small businesses or anybody else wanting to get started on the site. I both wrote and designed the book.
